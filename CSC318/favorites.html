FAVORITE HOBBIES

READING

Reading is one of my favorite hobbies, I always spend my free time with reading. I read various type of books genre such as romantic, war etc.
These are the list of one of my all the time favorite books;-
Moonlight on Linolium by Terry Helwig
A real life story about the writer herself.
The Perks of Being Wallflower by Stephen Chbosky
A story about a boy who his presence never bother anyone, his absent won't effect anything.
Love & Misadventure by Lang Leav
A poems book full with love poems.
The First Phone Call From Heaven by Mitch Albom
A story about Sully Harding, a grief-stricken single father is determined to find out about something that can be considered as miracle.

HIKING

Hiking is my most favorite yet rarely-I-do hobby. Whenever I have an absolute free time, I will go for hiking because I find it so relaxing and satisfying. I have climbed several hills across the country such as:-
  1. Bukit Batu Putih, Port Dickson
  2. Bukit Panorama, Kuantan
  3. Bukit Pelindung, Kuantan
  4. Bukit Broga, Semenyih
  5. Bukit Keluang, Terengganu
  6. Bukit Tabur, Kuala Lumpur
  7. Bukit Batu Condong, Kemensah
  8. Bukit Batu Lebah, Melaka

SINGING

Singing is also one of my favorite hobbies. I do it all the time, I sing when I'm bored, doing work, making food, etc. I really love to sing and I really love it when people enjoy listening to my voice.
